BONUS UNLOCK

How to unlock QV Breaker Station missions

The starter Shubin contract must be completed in Stanton. After that first mission unlocks the reputation track, the remaining reputation grind can be continued through Shubin contracts in Nyx, including the Levski delivery route in this guide.

Shared mining rights

Required rank: Jr. Contractor — 800 reputation

Complete the introductory mission plus the seven Step 2 deliveries: 8 total Shubin missions.

Contract: QV Breaker Station Mining Rights (Shared)

Entry cost: 175,000 aUEC

Other crews may receive access to the same station and its resources.

Exclusive mining rights

Required rank: Contractor — 2,200 reputation

After reaching Jr. Contractor, complete the fourteen Step 3 deliveries: 22 total Shubin missions including the intro.

Contract: QV Breaker Station Mining Rights (Exclusive)

Entry cost: 850,000 aUEC

Your group receives exclusive coordinates, though outside players may still discover the location.

Where to find the contracts

  1. Travel to the Nyx system.
  2. Open Contract Manager.
  3. Open the Ship Mining tab.
  4. Look for QV Breaker Station Mining Rights (Shared) or (Exclusive).
  5. Accept the version matching your rank and desired access type.

QV Breaker Stations are Operation-style missions in the Keeger Belt. The objective is to restore station power, restart its systems, align the mining laser, crack the captured asteroid, and harvest the resources inside.

STEP 1

Small Purchase Order: Hand Mined Materials

Stanton • Neutral • 45,250 aUEC • 100 rep

Deliver one populated requirement: 15 Aphorite, 20 Dolivine, or 5 Hadanite. Use the inventory kiosk first and place only the requested amount on the freight elevator.

After completion: 100 reputation and approximately 1,045,250 aUEC before material cost.

STEP 2

XS Purchase Order: Ship Mined Ore

Nyx • Neutral → Jr. Contractor • 23,000 aUEC • 100 rep

Keep the Levski mission requiring 2 SCU Aluminum. Complete 7 missions.

Buy 112 SCU Aluminum at Nyx Gateway for Steps 2 and 3 together: fourteen 8-SCU crates. Step 2 consumes 14 SCU and leaves 98 SCU.

Estimated ending balance: 1,043,178 aUEC.

STEP 3

Small Purchase Order: Ship Mined Ore

Nyx • Jr. Contractor → Contractor • 60,750 aUEC • 100 rep

Keep the Levski mission requiring 7 SCU Aluminum. Complete 14 missions, consuming the remaining 98 SCU.

Total payouts: 850,500 aUEC. Estimated ending balance: 1,893,678 aUEC.

STEP 4

Med. Purchase Order: Ship Mined Ore

Nyx • Contractor → Sr. Contractor • 48,250 aUEC • 150 rep

Deliver 11 SCU Aluminum per completion. Complete 24 missions: 264 SCU total, exactly thirty-three 8-SCU crates.

Planning material range: 383,856–916,080 aUEC. Estimated ending balance: 2,135,598–2,667,822 aUEC.

STEP 5

Lrg. Purchase Order: Ship Mined Ore

Nyx • Sr. Contractor → Veteran Contractor • 93,500 aUEC • 200 rep

Keep the Levski mission requiring 8 SCU Agricium. Complete 46 missions, requiring 368 SCU total.

Buy at Levski for convenience or compare Endgame in Pyro for possible savings. Recommended batching: four 80-SCU batches and one 48-SCU batch.

Estimated ending balance: 3,353,126–4,451,702 aUEC.

STEP 6

XL Purchase Order: Ship Mined Ore

Nyx • Veteran Contractor → Head Contractor • 321,250 aUEC • 250 rep
