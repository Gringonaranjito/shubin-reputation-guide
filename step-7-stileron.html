STEP 7B

Stileron Route

Head Contractor → Elite Contractor

Use the higher-paying final-stage material and complete 229 Major ship-mining purchase orders.

Open mission on SCMDB Check Stileron on UEX

Route note: Stileron pays more per mission than Quantanium, but it may take longer to locate consistently. Use this route when you already have a reliable Stileron source or enough refined stock available.

Reputation calculation

95,250 − 38,000 = 57,250 reputation needed

57,250 ÷ 250 = 229 missions

Total refined Stileron: 229 × 9 = 2,061 SCU.

Total gross payout: 229 × 405,750 = 92,916,750 aUEC.

Compared with an all-Quantanium route, this is 4,179,250 aUEC more gross payout.

Mining and refinery workflow

  1. Search for a suitable Stileron-bearing deposit.
  2. Fracture the rock and collect the valuable pieces.
  3. Return the raw ore to a refinery.
  4. Create refinery work orders and track only final refined output.
  5. Continue until at least 90 SCU is ready for a ten-mission batch.
  6. Collect completed orders and transport the refined cargo to Levski.
  7. Move it into Levski local inventory.

Get the correct mission

  1. Accept the Major ship-mining purchase order.
  2. Keep the populated mission requiring 9 SCU Stileron.
  3. Inspect the destination and keep Levski.
  4. Cancel other populated destinations and inspect the next mission.
  5. If Levski does not appear after many rerolls, switch servers and try again.

Levski personal-hangar loop

  1. Keep refined Stileron in Levski local inventory.
  2. Accept and verify the 9-SCU Stileron mission with Levski as destination.
  3. Bring the freight elevator up.
  4. Place the largest available Stileron crate or crates on it.
  5. Send the elevator down.
  6. The mission should accept exactly 9 SCU and return unused cargo to local inventory.
  7. Wait for confirmation, complete the mission, and repeat.

Do not manually split the full shipment into 9-SCU groups.
