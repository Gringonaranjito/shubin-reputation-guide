STEP 7A

Quantanium Route

Head Contractor → Elite Contractor

Use the easier-to-find final-stage material and complete 229 Major ship-mining purchase orders.

Open mission on SCMDB Check Quantanium on UEX

Important: Raw Quantanium is volatile after extraction. Refined Quantanium is stable and can be transported and stored normally.

Reputation calculation

Total refined Quantanium: 229 × 9 = 2,061 SCU.

Total gross payout: 229 × 387,500 = 88,737,500 aUEC.

Mining and refinery workflow

  1. Search for a suitable Quantanium-bearing deposit.
  2. Fracture the rock and extract the valuable pieces.
  3. Return to a refinery promptly after extraction.
  4. Create refinery work orders and count only the final refined output.
  5. Continue until at least 90 SCU is ready for a ten-mission batch.
  6. Collect the refinery orders and transport the refined cargo to Levski.
  7. Move it into Levski local inventory.

Get the correct mission

  1. Accept the Major ship-mining purchase order.
  2. Keep the populated mission requiring 9 SCU Quantanium.
  3. Inspect the destination and keep Levski.
  4. Cancel other populated destinations and inspect the next mission.
  5. If Levski does not appear after many rerolls, switch servers and try again.

Levski personal-hangar loop

  1. Keep refined Quantanium in Levski local inventory.
  2. Accept and verify the 9-SCU Quantanium mission with Levski as destination.
  3. Bring the freight elevator up.
  4. Place the largest available Quantanium crate or crates on it.
  5. Send the elevator down.
  6. The mission should accept exactly 9 SCU and return unused cargo to local inventory.
  7. Wait for confirmation, complete the mission, and repeat.

Do not manually split the full shipment into 9-SCU groups.
